HALAL EXPO 360°

What is Halal Expo 360° ?

Due to the world pandemic caused by COVID-19, Arabesque and the American Halal Council has established a partnership with Archytas Blockchain Consultancy & Technologies Corp, and Halal Life International Platform to build a virtual Halal Expo 360°.

The Halal Expo 360° platform is an advertising, B2B, and B2C platform that uses AR, to build a scalable supply chain management system to streamline operations, reduce operating costs, and faster growth. Combine smart contracts, blockchain, and AR to connect businesses and consumers.

The Halal Expo 360° utilizes the Halal Life International Platform as the first layer data as well as the data from other layers to hit precisely the targeted audience. When setting up an exhibition or campaign, tens of thousands of data segments will be available to fit the profile of the target audience. The platform allows businesses to create their own audience segments, allowing them to re-target specific audience segments with future exhibitions and campaigns.
